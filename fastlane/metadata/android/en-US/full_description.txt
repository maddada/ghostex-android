Ghostex Android connects your phone to the Ghostex sessions already running on your Mac.

The app opens a Termux-backed terminal surface, reaches your Mac over Tailscale SSH, asks the Ghostex CLI for the live session list, and attaches to ZMX-backed sessions so your work stays persistent on the main machine.

Core workflow:
* Save multiple SSH machines and switch between them from the Ghostex drawer.
* Reconnect automatically to the last selected machine when the app reopens.
* Open Tailscale from setup and recovery screens.
* Use the first-run setup guide to configure Tailscale, macOS Remote Login, Ghostex CLI, and ZMX.
* Keep recently opened remote sessions warm for faster switching.

Ghostex Android currently supports ZMX-backed Ghostex sessions only. The Android terminal is the attach surface; the persistent sessions continue to live on the Mac.
